1. Data scientists utilize their analytical, statistical, and programming skills to collect, analyze, and interpret large data sets.
  2. They then use this information to develop data-driven solutions to difficult business challenges.

  3. Data scientists commonly have a bachelor's degree in statistics, math, computer science, or economics.

  4. Data scientists have a wide range of technical competencies including statistics and machine learning, coding languages, databases, machine learning, and reporting technologies.

  5. They get an avarage of $97k - $113k.
